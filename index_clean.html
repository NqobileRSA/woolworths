About Us

Our Commitment to Wellness

Woolworths Wellness & Self-Care was founded in 2010 with a simple mission: to provide South Africans with high-quality, natural wellness products that enhance their daily self-care routines while respecting our planet.

We carefully source organic ingredients from ethical suppliers across South Africa, ensuring that every product meets our rigorous standards for purity and sustainability. Our commitment extends beyond products to our eco-friendly packaging and carbon-neutral shipping practices.

At Woolworths Wellness, we believe that true wellness comes from nurturing both body and soul, which is why we're dedicated to creating products that help you feel your best while making a positive impact on the world around you.
